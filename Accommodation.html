Hotel information

We recommend that participants stay at the AKA Hotel for convenience, as the conference will be held nearby. However, due to the limited capacity of the AKA Hotel, the number of available rooms for participants is restricted. We advise you to book your accommodation as soon as possible. If you are unable to secure a reservation at the AKA Hotel, there are other hotels nearby that you can consider.

Travel information

Conference Venue

Conference Venue: Center for Public and Business Administration Education (CPBAE)

Address: No. 187, Jinhua Street, Da’an District, Taipei City, Taiwan 106

Landmarks Nearby: Located near Dongmen Station and the Yongkang Street area.

Travel Tips: Ensure you have the address written in Chinese: 臺北市大安區金華街187號.

Use navigation apps like Google Maps for real-time directions.

Option 1: Airport MRT + Taipei Metro

Step 1: Take the Taoyuan Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station. Follow the signs to the Airport MRT station in Terminal 1 or Terminal 2. Take the Express Line (purple) for a faster trip (~35 minutes).

Step 2: From Taipei Main Station, transfer to the Taipei Metro (MRT). Follow signs to the Red Line (Tamsui-Xinyi Line). (You might need walk for few minutes) Board a train heading towards Xiangshan and alight at Dongmen Station. Travel Time: Approximately 5-10 minutes.

Step 3: From Dongmen Station to CPBAE. Exit the station via Exit 3. Walk south on Jinshan South Road for about 5 minutes. Turn left onto Jinhua Street; CPBAE is located at No. 187, Jinhua Street. Total Walking Time: Approximately 10 minutes.

Option 2: Direct Taxi

Taxis are available outside both terminals at the airport. Travel Time: 50–60 minutes (depending on traffic). Approximate Cost: 1,000–1,200 TWD (~32–38 USD).

Tip: Provide the driver with the address: 臺北市大安區金華街187號 (No. 187, Jinhua Street, Da’an District, Taipei City).

Option 3: Airport Bus + Taipei Metro

Step 1: Take an airport bus to Taipei Main Station. Buses are available outside the arrivals hall. Purchase a ticket at the bus counter. Travel Time: Approximately 55 minutes.

Step 2: From Taipei Main Station, follow the steps outlined in Option 1 to reach CPBAE via the Taipei Metro.
