The 37th Asian Finance Association
Annual Conference

July 1-3, 2025 (Mon-Wed), NCCU, Taiwan

Call for papers

The Asian Finance Association (AsianFA) welcomes submissions of finance research papers for its 2025 Annual Conference. The conference is scheduled to be held in Taipei from June 26th to June 28th, 2025. The conference is organized by the Asian Finance Association and co-organized by the National Chengchi University. The conference provides an open platform for finance scholars worldwide to present their recent research and exchange ideas on new developments in finance research. The majority of the sessions will include papers selected through open competition. The papers will be reviewed by a committee of established researchers.

Keynote speakers

1. Clemens Sialm => Professor and Department Chair of Finance, University of Texas at Austin

2.Jaewon Choi => Professor of Finance, Department of Economics, Seoul National University

Topics

The theme of the conference is on frontiers of modern finance research. Papers in all areas of finance are welcome, including asset pricing, behavioral finance, corporate finance, emerging capital markets, financial institutions, fintech, international finance, market microstructure, etc. Accepted papers are eligible for various categories of best paper awards.

Best paper awards

The conference will offer best paper awards. The awards will be decided by an Award Committee. These awards include: 1. Three best paper awards (USD 2,000 each). 2. Three excellent paper awards (USD 1,000 each).
